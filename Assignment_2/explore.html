FOOD FARM HUB
India has a rich agricultural heritage, with organic farming emerging as a prominent practice in recent years. Explore the vibrant landscape of organic farming communities in India, delving into both the advantages and disadvantages associated with this agricultural approach.
The history of agriculture in India dates back to the  Neolithic period . India  ranks  second worldwide in farm outputs. As per the Indian economic survey 2020 -21, agriculture employed more than 50% of the Indian workforce and contributed 20.2% to the country's GDP.
In a world battered by the COVID pandemic, the growth story of organic farming is unfolding with increasing demand not only in India but also globally.
